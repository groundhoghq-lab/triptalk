Help

Keyboard controls and touch/click actions for TripTalk.

Center cardStart the selected section, then advance to the next card.
Side cards / dotsSwitch sections and show that section's title card.
Transport buttonsMove by section or card, and play, pause, or resume target-language audio.
MenuOpen Settings, Pronunciation, Help, and About.
EnterAdvance to the next card.
Arrow Left / Arrow RightBefore card mode, change sections. In card mode, move previous or next card.
End of sectionNext from the last card opens the next section. Previous from the first card opens the previous section.
Shift + ArrowIn card mode, change sections instead of changing cards.
, and .Use comma and period like left and right arrows.
< and >Use shifted comma and period like Shift + Arrow for section changes.
SpaceStart from section view, or play, pause, and resume target-language audio in card view.
RReset the current card and rewind its audio. If English is on, English plays again.
EToggle automatic English playback when cards appear.
CToggle Continuous Playback. In card mode, English finishes, waits 2 seconds, then Greek plays, waits 2 seconds, and advances to the next card. If English is off, Greek plays and waits 2 seconds before advancing.
GToggle Global Mode. Global Mode combines every section into one deck; when Shuffle is on, all cards are shuffled together.
[ / ]Decrease or increase the Continuous Playback pause from English to Greek. When English is off, this controls the pause from one Greek phrase to the next.
+ / -Speed up or slow down target-language audio by 5%, from 75% to 150%.
SToggle shuffled card order on or off. New sections use the current shuffle setting.
IShow the TripTalk about screen.
PToggle the Greek pronunciation chart on or off.
H / ?Toggle this help panel on or off. Escape also closes it.
